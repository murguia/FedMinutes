Minutes of a meeting of members of the Board of Governors of 
the Federal Reserve System on Monday, January 10, 1972. The meeting 
was held in the Board Room at 10:00 a.m.

The research staff presented a review of foreign exchange 
developments and domestic business and financial conditions of the kind 
customarily given in advance of meetings of the Federal Open Market 
Committee. The reports were based to some extent on materials that had 
been prepared for the meeting of the Committee scheduled for tomorrow, 
and copies of the relevant documentation have been placed in the 
Committee's files. 
The meeting then adjourned. 
1/ Other officers and staff of the Division also were present
