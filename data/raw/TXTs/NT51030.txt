The regular weekly economic and financial review was presented. 
Copies of the statistical materials prepared for the review have been 
placed in the Board's files, along with copies of the individual briefings. 
Mr. Rippey reported on legislative developments. 
Chairman Burns then called for an executive session of the 
Board and all staff members except Messrs. Holland and Bryant withdrew. 
The Chairman reported that, in connection with confidential U.S. 
Governmental discussions as to what if anything could and should be done 
by the U.S. in view of current developments in international financial
