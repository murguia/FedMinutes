In keeping with customary procedure, and in preparation for 
the meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee to be held next 
Tuesday, staff presented a review of domestic and international financial 
developments. Copies of the relevant statistical materials and of the 
individual briefings have been placed in the Board's files. 
Discussion relative to the staff review had to do mainly with 
the indicated substantial rate of growth in the money supply and other 
monetary aggregates for the month of May. Views were exchanged on
