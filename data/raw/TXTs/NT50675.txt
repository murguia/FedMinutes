Economic and financial review. 
The usual weekly economic 
and financial review was presented. 
Copies of the statistical charts 
and tables prepared for the review have been placed in the Board's 
files, along with copies of the individual briefings; these were also 
made available to all Board members. 
Legislative review. Mr. Cardon brought the Board members 
up to date on the status of several pending bills. 
1/ Other officers and staff of the Division also were present.

Discount rates. 
The establishment without change by the 
Federal Reserve Banks of Cleveland, Richmond, Atlanta, Chicago, 
St. Louis, Minneapolis, Kansas City, and Dallas on September 24, 1970, 
of the rates on discounts and advances in their existing schedules was 
approved unanimously, with the understanding that appropriate advice 
would be sent to those Banks. 
The meeting then adjourned.
