Minutes of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve 
System on Friday, May 28, 1971. 
The Board met in the Board Room 
at 10:00 a.m. 
PRESENT: 
Mr 
Robertson, Vice Chairman 
Mr. Maisel 
Mr. Brimmer 
Mr. Sherrill

1/ 
Mr. Ghiardi, Adviser, Division of International 
Finance 
1/ 
Mrs. Koester, Technical Assistant, Office of the

The regular weekly economic and financial review was presented. 
Copies of the statistical charts and tables prepared for the review have 
been placed in the Board's files along with copies of the individual 
briefings. 
Mr. Cardon then briefed the Board on matters pertinent to the 
Federal Reserve presently being considered by the Congress. 
The meeting then adjourned.
