The usual weekly economic and financial review was presented; 
copies of the individual staff briefings and statistical tables pre-
pared for the purpose of the review have been placed in the Board's 
files. 
Comments also were made regarding developments in the commer-
cial paper market, particularly with respect to bank financing being 
arranged for a finance company.
