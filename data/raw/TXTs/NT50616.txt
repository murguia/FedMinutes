The usual weekly economic and financial review was presented.

have been placed in the Board's files, along with copies of the individ-

After discussion based on the review, Mr. Holland commented 
briefly on his meeting yesterday with the directors of the Minneapolis 
Reserve Bank. 
There being no further business, the meeting then adjourned.
