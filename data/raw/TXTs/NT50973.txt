First National City. On October 5, 1972, the Board approved the 
application of First National City Corporation, New York, New York, to 
acquire shares of Citicorp International Bank Limited, London, England. 
In approving the application the Board had relied on an oral understanding 
with senior officers of the organization concerning a program to increase 
the equity capital of First National City Bank of New York in 1972 and 1973.

Prior to today's meeting a draft letter rescinding approval of the application 
had been distributed, since there apparently had been a misunderstanding with 
respect to the capital improvement program for 1973. 
Discussion developed a consensus for revising the letter to indicate 
that the Board would not reconsider the application until it had been informed 
in writing of plans for additions to the equity capital of First National City 
Bank, and that pending receipt of this information the Board would withhold 
Judgment regarding the capital position of the bank. 
The Board thereupon approved the letter rescinding its approval action 
of October 5, with the understanding that it would be revised as suggested 
a t today's meeting. A copy of the letter in the form sent to First National 
City Corporation is attached as Item No. 5.

Gentlemen: 
In its letter of October 6, 1972, the Board indicated its 
approval of an application by First National City Corporation "to 
Purchase and hold all of the shares of Citicorp International Bank 
Limited ("CIBL"), London, England, at a cost: of approximately 
$5 >200,000." In approving this application the Board relied upon 
what it believed to be an oral understanding with senior officers 
your organization concerning a program to increase equity capital * 
First National City Bank in 1972 and 1973. However, there appears 
to have been a misunderstanding with respect to the program for 1973. 
In view of this misunderstanding, our letter of October 6, 
72 is superseded by this one. and the Board hereby rescinds its 
action approving the above-mentioned application. Before reconsid-
ering the application we should be informed in writing of your plans 
for additions to the equity capital of First National City Bank. 
please so inform us as early as feasible. Pending receipt thereof, 
c'ne Board will withhold judgment regarding the capital position of 
-irst National City Bank.
