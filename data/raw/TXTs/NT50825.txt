Minutes of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve 
System on Monday, September 20, 1971. The Board met in the Board Room 
at 10:00 a.m.

Staff presented a review of domestic and international developments 
of the kind customarily given in preparation for meetings of the Federal 
Open Market Committee. Copies of the statistical materials prepared for 
this review and of the individual papers presented have been placed in 
the Board's files. 
At the conclusion of the review, the meeting continued with 
limited staff attendance.
